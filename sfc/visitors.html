Welcome to the Visitor area for the San Francisco Resistance team.

We're happy you're here and would love to help you play with and meet our team. If you're new to Ingress, check out our new players area.

We have an invitation-only visitor-focused Google Hangout which you are welcome to join. If we know that there are active and interested visitors in town, we'll work with you there to coordinate builds, social events, and local knowledge, Ingress or not.

If you're going to be here for an extended stay, feel free to sign up to be verified for access to our full Slack team.

You can get an invitation to our Visitor's hangout several ways:

  • Join our "Welcome to Ingress Hangout" and ask for an invite (please let us know your agent name and any references!).
  • Ask one of our outreach team members on faction COMM.
